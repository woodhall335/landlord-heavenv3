# High-Intent Pages Report — One Pager

## Executive Take
Landlord Heaven has a credible opportunity to grow high-intent SEO traffic and revenue, but outcomes depend on disciplined execution of canonical strategy and funnel routing.

## What traffic can we expect?

### Repo-backed baseline (direct)
- **~2,000 monthly visitors** projected from **4 new info pages**.
- **~11,000 monthly SEO visitors** projected overall when the wider SEO plan is implemented.

### Practical operating expectation (estimate)
- **Months 1–2:** ~400–1,200 monthly visits total across the 4 pages.
- **After ranking gains:** ~1,400–2,800 monthly visits total.
- **Strong ranking scenario:** 2,000–4,000+ monthly visits total.

> Estimate notes: ranges are inferred from internal projections, rollout timing, and typical ranking ramp—not external volume data.

## Are the pages detailed enough to index strongly?
Yes, on paper.

- Planned depth is substantial:
  - `/section-21-notice` = **2,000+ words**
  - `/section-8-notice` = **2,000+ words**
  - `/eviction-notice-uk` = **1,500+ words**
- Platform already has strong SEO building blocks (metadata tooling, schema support, broad sitemap coverage).
- Discoverability support is in place via an expanded dynamic sitemap with many high-intent landing pages.

## Main risks/blockers
1. **Canonical inconsistencies** in high-intent clusters can split authority.
2. **Funnel fragmentation** can keep users in guide/tool loops instead of paid pathways.
3. **Inconsistent CTA routing** reduces conversion quality and attribution clarity.
4. **Cannibalization risk** across overlapping Section 21/8 and eviction pages.
5. **Stale freshness signals** if legal-content update cadence is not maintained.
6. **Problem-aware and court-stage content gaps** still leave demand uncaptured.

## What to do next (top 5)
1. Set canonical primary URLs for each intent cluster and enforce internally.
2. Standardize journey: **SEO page → product page → wizard → checkout**.
3. Publish high-depth priority pages with strict schema + conversion blocks.
4. Fix internal-link dead ends and ensure first CTA drives product pathway.
5. Add freshness + funnel analytics governance (page→wizard→checkout tracking).

## Bottom-line verdict
- **Worth investing?** Yes.
- **Detailed enough?** Yes, if content specs are executed and linked correctly.
- **Realistic outcome?** Strong chance of meaningful organic growth and improved conversion efficiency, with performance largely determined by funnel coherence and canonical discipline.
