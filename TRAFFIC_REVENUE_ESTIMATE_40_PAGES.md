# Traffic & Revenue Estimate for 40 High-Intent Eviction Pages

## Scope
This estimate covers the 40 URLs listed across:
- Core eviction guides
- Process guides
- Rent arrears / tenant problem pages
- Notice-stage pages
- Long-tail high-intent pages

## Modelling assumptions (realistic, UK legal-intent SEO)
- Content quality: pages are now materially longer/deeper, with process intent and FAQ coverage.
- Ranking ramp: slower in first 2-3 months, stronger after internal links and query data feedback.
- Click-through and conversion behaviour:
  - Organic-to-product click rate: **8-14%**
  - Product-to-purchase conversion: **10-18%**
  - Blended page-session-to-purchase conversion: **1.0-2.5%** (base: **1.8%**)
- Blended average order value (AOV): **£34**
  - Based on a mix of Notice Only (£19.99), Money Claim (~£34.99), and Complete Pack (£49.99).

## Traffic estimate

### 30-day traffic (next 30 days)
- **Conservative:** 1,500 sessions
- **Base (realistic):** 2,500 sessions
- **Upside:** 4,000 sessions

### 12-month traffic
Two views are useful:

1) **12-month cumulative traffic (total sessions over the next year)**
- **Conservative:** 80,000
- **Base (realistic):** 125,000
- **Upside:** 190,000

2) **Month-12 run-rate (sessions in month 12 alone)**
- **Conservative:** 9,000 / month
- **Base (realistic):** 14,000 / month
- **Upside:** 22,000 / month

## Revenue estimate
Formula used:

`Revenue = Organic Sessions × Blended Conversion Rate × Blended AOV`

### 30-day revenue
- **Conservative:** 1,500 × 1.2% × £32 = **£576**
- **Base (realistic):** 2,500 × 1.8% × £34 = **£1,530**
- **Upside:** 4,000 × 2.4% × £36 = **£3,456**

### 12-month revenue (cumulative year)
- **Conservative:** 80,000 × 1.2% × £32 = **£30,720**
- **Base (realistic):** 125,000 × 1.8% × £34 = **£76,500**
- **Upside:** 190,000 × 2.4% × £36 = **£164,160**

## Quick interpretation
- A **realistic planning case** is:
  - **30-day traffic:** ~2,500
  - **30-day revenue:** ~£1.5k
  - **12-month cumulative traffic:** ~125k
  - **12-month cumulative revenue:** ~£76.5k
- If rankings improve faster (better internal links, stronger CTR snippets, and clear CTA routing), upside can be materially higher.
