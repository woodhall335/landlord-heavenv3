Stage 2: Section 8 Court & Possession Pack
What Happens Next
READY FOR COURT
Prepare and issue the possession claim using the forms and guidance in this pack once the claim is eligible on or
after 28 May 2026.
STEP 1
Issue the claim when eligible
Issue the possession claim once the notice period has expired and no earlier than 28 May 2026.
STEP 2
Prepare for the hearing
At the hearing, the court will consider notice validity, service, whether the grounds apply, and whether the arrears
are proved. Keep the file updated to the latest practicable date.
STEP 3
Move to possession or enforcement
If possession is granted, follow the date to leave in the order. If the tenant does not leave, move straight into
warrant or enforcement steps using the same continuous file.
After court preparation
Keep the issued claim, service record, hearing bundle, and any later enforcement papers aligned in the same case
file.
This pack completes the case started in Stage 1 and is structured to be used as a single continuous file. The
stronger the continuity between notice, claim, hearing, and enforcement, the less room there is for avoidable
procedural challenge.
