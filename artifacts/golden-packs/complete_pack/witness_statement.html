A detailed Schedule of Arrears (Exhibit DM1) showing the period-by-period breakdown of rent due and payments received is attached to this pack. I will provide an updated Schedule of Arrears to the court before the hearing to confirm the arrears position at that date. Ground 10 is a discretionary ground which applies where some rent is unpaid both at the date of the notice and at the date of proceedings. The court has discretion to make a possession order if it considers it reasonable to do so.
