The deposit may be used at the end of the tenancy for:
1. Unpaid rent and other charges under the tenancy agreement
2. Damage beyond fair wear and tear to:
- Property
- Fixtures and fittings
- Furnishings
3. Cleaning costs if the property is not returned reasonably clean
4. Loss of keys or other security devices
5. Other breaches of the tenancy agreement
