If payment is late, the landlord should record the missed payment clearly and keep the rent record up to

The tenant should raise any payment issue promptly so the file reflects what has happened in real time.
Persistent arrears may affect later possession grounds, so the payment history should stay aligned with

4. Issue Escalation Framework
Missed inspections, access refusals, or repeated difficulty arranging contractor attendance should be
recorded in writing.
If repairs are not reported promptly, the landlord should record what was known, when, and what was
done next.
Repeated breach of agreed management rules should be logged clearly with the date, the issue, and the
written follow-up.
Where an issue arises, the landlord should record it clearly, communicate in writing, and keep the tenancy
file updated.
