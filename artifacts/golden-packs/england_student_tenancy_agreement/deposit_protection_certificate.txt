WHAT THIS MEANS
Protection
Your deposit is protected in a government-approved scheme. This means: - Your deposit is safe -
You can challenge unfair deductions - There is a free dispute resolution service - The landlord faces
penalties if they don't protect your deposit
At End of Tenancy
When the tenancy ends: 1. You and the landlord will agree deposit deductions (if any) 2. If you
agree, the deposit will be returned (minus agreed deductions) 3. If you disagree, you can use the
free Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) service 4. The ADR decision is binding on both parties
Disputes
If there is a dispute about deductions: - Contact the scheme's ADR service - Submit evidence
(photos, inventory, receipts) - An independent adjudicator will decide - The decision is usually made
within 28 days - Both parties must accept the decision
