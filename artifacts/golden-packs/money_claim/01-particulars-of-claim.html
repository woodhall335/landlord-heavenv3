Particulars of Claim

Claimant: Alice Landlord

Defendant: Tom Tenant

Property: 2 High Street London

Tenancy background

The Defendant occupies the above property under an assured shorthold tenancy that commenced on 1 January 2024. Rent is £950.00 monthly, payable in advance on the 1 of each period.

The Claimant seeks judgment for the unpaid rent, together with any properly recoverable damage, charges, interest, and court fee set out below.

Rent arrears

The Defendant has failed to pay rent as it fell due. As at 23 April 2026, the arrears total is £2430.00. A detailed schedule is attached and marked "Schedule of arrears".

Property damage and other sums

The Defendant caused damage beyond fair wear and tear. The Claimant seeks:

  • Broken internal door - £200.00
  • Professional clean due to excessive staining - £180.00

The Claimant also seeks the following recoverable sums:

  • Locksmith call-out - £80.00

Interest

The Claimant claims interest pursuant to section 69 County Courts Act 1984 at 8% per annum from 2 February 2026 to 23 April 2026, calculated at £50.40, and continuing at £0.63 per day until judgment or earlier payment.

Relief sought

  1. Arrears, damages and other sums totalling £2940.40
  2. Court fee of £205.00
  3. Interest under section 69 CCA 1984

The Claimant asks the court to enter judgment for those sums and for any continuing daily interest claimed until judgment or earlier payment.
