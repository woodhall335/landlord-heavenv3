READY FOR COURT - CLAIM FILE ALIGNED
Prepare and issue the possession claim using the forms and guidance in this pack once the claim is
eligible on or after 3 April 2026.
STEP 1
Issue the claim when eligible
Issue the possession claim once the notice period has expired and no earlier than 3 April 2026.
STEP 2
Prepare for the hearing
At the hearing, the court will consider notice validity, service, whether the grounds apply, and whether the
arrears are proved. Keep the file updated to the latest practicable date.
STEP 3
Move to possession or enforcement
If possession is granted, follow the date to leave in the order. If the tenant does not leave, move straight
into warrant or enforcement steps using the same continuous file.
After court preparation
Keep the issued claim, service record, hearing bundle, and any later enforcement papers aligned in the
same case file.
This pack continues the case started with the notice. The more clearly the notice, claim, hearing bundle,
and enforcement papers line up, the less room there is for avoidable challenge.
