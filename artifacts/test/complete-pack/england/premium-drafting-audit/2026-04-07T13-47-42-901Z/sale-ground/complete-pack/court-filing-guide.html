The claimant seeks possession of 16 Willow Mews York YO24 3HX relying on Ground 1A (Sale of dwelling house).

Form 3A notice served on 1 June 2026, expiring on 1 October 2026, with proceedings not intended before 1 October 2026.

If the matter is not resolved after service of the Form 3A notice, the claim should be prepared for filing in York County Court and Family Court using the same factual basis and dates as the notice pack.

Before Issue

  • Review the notice, proof of service, witness statement, and claim forms together before issue.
  • Check that the same court name, notice timeline, and grounds appear consistently across the pack.
  • Check that the evidence still proves the statutory ground on the date proceedings are issued.

What To File

  • File Form N5, Form N119, the notice, and proof of service together with any required fee or fee-remission material.
  • Prepare an indexed bundle containing the tenancy documents, the served notice, service evidence, and the ground-specific documents relied on.
  • Ownership proof
  • Sale intention statement
  • Sale evidence

After Issue

  • Monitor for any defence, witness evidence, or counterclaim and update the bundle so the response documents answer it directly.
  • If circumstances materially change after issue, update the figures or factual chronology promptly and reflect that change consistently across the pack.
  • Before the hearing, re-check every court-facing document for blank placeholders, stale dates, or unsupported allegations.

The court is likely to expect contemporary evidence of the proposed occupation, sale, or other intended use.

Do not allow the claim forms to drift into generic wording; each pleaded ground should still read as fact-specific and evidence-led at issue and at hearing.
