# Accessibility Review

## Checks

- One H1 emitted by the shared shell.
- Logical H2 hierarchy for article sections.
- Related resources are rendered as descriptive link cards.
- External links use natural, meaningful anchor text.
- No icon-only links were added.
- No colour-only status information was added.
- FAQ content remains text-based and accessible through the existing FAQ component.

## Verdict

PASS. The changes are semantic and text-first.

## Minor Future Improvement

If the site later adds article table-of-contents navigation, use a real nav landmark with skip-friendly anchors.
