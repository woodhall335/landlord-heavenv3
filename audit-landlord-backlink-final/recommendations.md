# Recommendations

## Implement First

Deploy the current QA-improved version as a conservative editorial backlink bridge.

## Monitor

After deployment, monitor:

- Google indexing of the five new support articles.
- Organic impressions for property business staffing queries.
- HRHeaven referral clicks from Landlord Heaven.
- Any Search Console warnings for duplicate titles, canonicals, or schema.

## Do Not Do

- Do not add HRHeaven links to the global footer, nav, sidebars, checkout, dashboard, wizard, or generated documents.
- Do not bulk-inject HRHeaven links into all blog templates.
- Do not repeat exact-match anchors across many pages.

## Later Improvements

- Add selected internal links from high-level hubs only after the pages are indexed.
- Consider one or two additional truly useful articles later, such as caretakers, HMO cleaners, or staff offboarding with keys and tenant data.
- Fix the existing analytics test-harness issue so SEO tests do not fail on `/api/analytics/events` relative URL fetches.
