# New Content Opportunities

Audit status: recommendations only. No content created.

## Why New Content Is The Better Play

Some existing Landlord Heaven pages can carry safe HRHeaven links, but the cleanest SEO path is to create a few landlord-business operations articles where HR is genuinely part of the topic. These pages can rank for long-tail queries and link naturally to HRHeaven without forcing HR links into eviction or tenancy pages.
