# Pages To Avoid

Audit status: recommendations only. No links added.

## Avoid Sitewide And Template Links

Do not add HRHeaven links to:

- global footer
- global header
- sidebar navigation
- wizard side panel
- checkout pages
- dashboard pages
- document download pages
- generic CTA components
- every blog template by default

Reason: this would create a sitewide or semi-sitewide cross-domain pattern that looks promotional rather than editorial.

## Avoid Core Eviction Notice Pages Unless Staff Context Exists

Avoid HRHeaven links on pages where the user intent is purely possession, notice periods, or court forms:

- `/products/notice-only`
- `/products/complete-pack`
- `/form-3-section-8`
- `/section-8-notice-template`
- `/n5-n119-possession-claim`
- `/eviction-court-forms-england`
- `/section-8-grounds-explained`
- most `/section-8-grounds/how-to-evict-a-tenant-using-ground-*` pages

Exception:

- A future Ground 16 page is a strong fit because it is directly about employee accommodation.
- Ground 13/14 pages may mention cleaners/contractors as evidence, but that alone is not enough for a direct HR link unless the page also discusses employing those people.

## Avoid Transactional Wizard And Checkout Pages

Do not add HR links to:

- `/wizard/*`
- `/wizard/review`
- checkout routes
- success pages
- dashboard case pages

Reason: the user is trying to complete a legal/property document workflow. HR links would distract and could look like cross-domain funnel manipulation.

## Avoid Thin Cleaning Mentions

Do not link from every page that says “cleaning”.

Avoid or treat as conditional:

- `/money-claim-bathroom-damage`
- `/money-claim-appliance-damage`
- `/money-claim-abandoned-goods`
- `/tenant-left-without-paying-rent`
- `/eviction-cost-uk`
- `/bailiff-eviction-process`
- `/court-bailiff-eviction-guide`

Reason: many of these pages mention cleaning as a recoverable cost or practical action, not as an employment/staffing topic.

## Avoid Retired Or Weak Jurisdiction Pages

Be cautious with:

- `/tenancy-agreements/wales`
- `/tenancy-agreements/northern-ireland`
- older Scotland/Wales/NI update pages
- deprecated AST or Section 21 pages

Reason: previous site discussions suggest some non-England flows were retired or problematic. Do not use them for backlink building until the product and compliance position is confirmed.

## Avoid Exact-Match Repetition Pages

Avoid placing the same destination and same anchor on multiple near-duplicate pages, especially:

- multiple Section 8 ground pages
- multiple money claim damage pages
- multiple rent arrears pages

Reason: repeated cross-domain anchors from similar pages can look engineered.

## Avoid Legal Advice Pages Where HR Is Off-Topic

Avoid HRHeaven links on pages focused on:

- deposit protection
- gas safety
- EPC
- Section 13 rent increases
- notice date calculators
- possession timetable pages
- court fee tables

Reason: no meaningful HR overlap.
