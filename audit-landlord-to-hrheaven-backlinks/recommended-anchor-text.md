# Recommended Anchor Text

Audit status: recommendations only. No links added.

## Anchor Principles

Use plain-English anchors that fit the sentence. Do not repeat the same exact-match anchor across many pages. Avoid keyword stuffing. Avoid brand anchors unless the context is a resources list or a clearly labelled partner/resource mention.

Best pattern:

> If you employ staff in the property business, keep the tenancy paperwork separate from your employment documents, such as an employment contract for property managers.

Avoid:

> Best property manager employment contract HRHeaven UK.

- `HR Heaven`
- `best HR documents`

## Placement Style

Keep the link in a practical aside or “related business paperwork” note.

Example:

> If you run the property through a company or employ staff, keep landlord documents separate from employment paperwork. HRHeaven has HR documents for property businesses, including contracts for property managers and letting agency staff.

Use one link in that paragraph, not three.
