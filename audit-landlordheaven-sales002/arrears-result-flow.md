# Rent-arrears result flow

- The free calculation remains visible.
- One Money Claim continuation follows a non-zero result.
- The handoff distinguishes calculation from paid document preparation.
- Only categorical arrears/journey bands are retained; no names, addresses, payment history, case narrative, or raw arrears value is sent in URLs or analytics.
- Edit returns to the calculator.
- `result_viewed`, offer view, and offer click are persisted.
- Visual inspection at the three mandated widths is blocked by the browser-runtime failure.
