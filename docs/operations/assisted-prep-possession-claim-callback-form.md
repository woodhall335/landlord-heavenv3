## Opening Script

Use plain wording:

> Thanks for booking Possession Claim Assisted Prep. I am going to check the key facts, what notice has been served, what evidence you have, and whether the file is ready for the possession claim pack. If something is missing or the timing is not ready, we will explain what is needed next. If the pack is unsuitable or we cannot reasonably proceed, we will offer the refund promised for assisted prep.
