## Minimum Standard

Do not start the rewrite until this brief is filled in well enough to answer:

- What page is being rewritten?
- What is the single primary search intent?
- What must remain true?
- What is the commercial goal?
- What tone should the page use?

If those answers are missing, the rewrite is not ready.
